# Step 2: User Personas & Journeys

Now that we have the core features, let's understand WHO will use them and HOW.

## Instructions

Guide the user through persona discovery:

> Now let's talk about your users! Understanding WHO uses the app helps us design better experiences.
>
> Tell me about your primary user:
> 1. Who are they? (role, technical level)
> 2. What's their main goal?
> 3. How often will they use this?

For each persona, document:

### Persona Template
```markdown
## Persona: {Name}

**Role**: {description}
**Technical Level**: {novice | intermediate | expert}
**Primary Goal**: {what they want to achieve}
**Usage Frequency**: {daily | weekly | occasional}

### User Journey
1. {step 1} → {feeling/expectation}
2. {step 2} → {feeling/expectation}
3. {step 3} → {feeling/expectation}

### Pain Points
- {frustration 1}
- {frustration 2}
```

## Completion

Ask: "Any other user types we should consider?"

When done, append to `01_requirements.md` under a new section "User Personas".
